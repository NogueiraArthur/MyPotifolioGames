HOW FAST ARE YOU?

This is a 2D game, top-down where your goal is to get as many points as possible
and as it is a scoring game he has only one level, your character is an elf with a bow that the
more enemies he kills the more enemies appear but they are also stronger with more
powerful arrows, the interesting thing about this game is his mechanics, that you have
to click where you want to walk but you also have to click where you want
to shoot, and I made this mechanic thinking about lol's kiting system.
