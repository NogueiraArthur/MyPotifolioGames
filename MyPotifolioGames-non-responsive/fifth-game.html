ARE YOUR MAGIC POWERFUL ENOUGH?

This is a 2D top-down game with a dungeon crawler style that I did together with
my friend Leonardo Gomes, I did all the programming and he did all the sprites except
for the spells we got from the internet, the game is set in a magic training camp and the apprentice
mage Azure is training there normally until the Thief that steals the three elemental runes of the
ancient mages and without them the camp is unprotected so Azure's goal is to go after the Thief
in the dugeon that he hid to retrieve the elemental runes.
